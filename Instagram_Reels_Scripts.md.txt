# Introduction

# Best Practices for Instagram Reels

- Keep videos between 15–30 seconds.
- Use natural lighting for better visuals.
- Speak in a conversational tone.
- Add trending background music.
- Include captions for accessibility.
- End with a clear and simple CTA.
- Show the product naturally during the video.
