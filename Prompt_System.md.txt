## AI Content Marketing using UGC Ads
### Prompt Engineering Task 2 (2026)

# Introduction

This document contains a reusable prompt engineering system designed to generate authentic User Generated Content (UGC) advertisements using Artificial Intelligence. The prompts are structured to create engaging, natural, and conversion-focused content for different products and businesses.

For this project, the selected product is **Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash**, but the prompts can easily be modified for any other product or service by replacing the product details.

---

# Master Prompt

**Prompt:**

You are an experienced UGC content creator and digital marketing copywriter.

Create a natural and authentic UGC advertisement for the following product.

**Product:** Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash

The advertisement should:
- Sound like a real customer sharing their experience.
- Follow the Problem → Solution → Benefits → Call-to-Action framework.
- Avoid overly promotional language.
- Be suitable for Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.
- Keep the tone friendly, trustworthy, and conversational.
- Generate a script between 30–45 seconds.

---

# Hook Generation Prompt

**Prompt:**

Generate 15 unique and attention-grabbing hooks for the product **Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash**.

Requirements:
- Maximum 10 words.
- Suitable for Instagram Reels.
- Create curiosity.
- Encourage viewers to continue watching.
- Avoid clickbait.

Example Output:
- Stop scrolling if your skin looks dull!
- I finally found a face wash that works!
- This skincare mistake ruined my glow...
- My tan disappeared faster than I expected!
