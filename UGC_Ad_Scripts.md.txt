# UGC Ad Scripts
## AI Content Marketing using UGC Ads
### Product: Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash

---

# Introduction

User Generated Content (UGC) advertisements are one of the most effective forms of digital marketing because they feel authentic and relatable. Instead of sounding like traditional advertisements, UGC scripts imitate genuine customer experiences, helping brands build trust and improve engagement. The following scripts are designed for **Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash** and follow the **Hook → Problem → Solution → Benefits → Call-to-Action (CTA)** framework.

---

# UGC Script 1 – Everyday Glow

**Hook:**  
"Stop scrolling if you're tired of dull, tanned skin!"

**Problem:**  
I spend a lot of time outdoors, and my skin started looking dull and uneven. I tried several face washes, but none gave noticeable results.

**Solution:**  
Then I started using **Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash**. It contains natural ingredients like Turmeric and Saffron, which gently cleanse the skin while helping remove tan.

**Benefits:**  
After using it consistently for two weeks, my skin felt cleaner, brighter, and much fresher. It also has a pleasant fragrance and is gentle enough for daily use.

**CTA:**  
If you're looking for a natural skincare product, give Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash a try. Your skin will thank you!
