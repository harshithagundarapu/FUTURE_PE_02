# Best Practices for Writing Effective CTAs

To maximize conversions, a CTA should:
- Be short and easy to understand.
- Use action-oriented words such as "Shop," "Try," "Discover," or "Order."
- Match the objective of the advertisement.
- Create a sense of urgency when appropriate.
- Encourage the audience to take immediate action.
- Sound natural and conversational rather than overly promotional.

Using different CTA variations allows marketers to test which messages perform best for different audiences and platforms.
