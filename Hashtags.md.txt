# Best Practices for Using Hashtags

To maximize engagement and reach:
- Use 8–15 relevant hashtags per Instagram post.
- Combine broad, niche, and branded hashtags.
- Avoid using unrelated or misleading hashtags.
- Rotate hashtags across different posts to reach new audiences.
- Place hashtags at the end of the caption for better readability.
- Track hashtag performance and update your strategy regularly.
