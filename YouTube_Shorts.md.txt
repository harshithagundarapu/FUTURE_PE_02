# Introduction

YouTube Shorts has become one of the fastest-growing platforms for short-form video marketing. Brands use Shorts to showcase products through quick, engaging, and relatable content that captures viewers' attention within the first few seconds. UGC (User Generated Content) advertisements perform particularly well because they feel like honest recommendations instead of traditional commercials.

The following scripts are designed for **Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash** using a storytelling approach that highlights real-life skincare concerns, product benefits, and a natural call-to-action.

---

# YouTube Short 1 – "My Everyday Skincare Essential"

**Duration:** 30 Seconds

### Hook
"Here's the one product I never skip in my skincare routine!"

"I spend a lot of time outdoors, so my skin often looks dull and tired. I wanted something gentle that could clean my face without making it dry. That's when I started using Mamaearth Ubtan Face Wash. It removes dirt, excess oil, and leaves my skin feeling fresh every morning. Plus, it contains natural ingredients like Turmeric and Saffron. It's now a part of my daily routine!"

### CTA
"Give it a try if you're looking for a simple skincare upgrade."

# Tips for High-Performing YouTube Shorts

- Grab attention within the first 3 seconds.
- Use a conversational and friendly tone.
- Show the product naturally during the video.
- Keep the script between 30–40 seconds.
- Include captions to improve accessibility.
- Focus on solving one specific customer problem.
- End with a simple and clear call-to-action.
